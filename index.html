Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a type of alternative medicine that focuses on the physical manipulation of the body's muscle tissue and bones.

Chiropractic

Chiropractic therapy is a treatment focused on diagnosing and manually adjusting spinal and musculoskeletal misalignments to relieve pain and improve overall body function.

IASTM Mobilization

IASTM is a form of manual therapy where special tools are used to manipulate and mobilize soft tissues.

Manual Therapy

Manual therapy is a hands-on treatment technique used by therapists to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore function in muscles and joints.

Kinesio Taping

Kinesio Taping is a therapeutic taping technique designed to support and stabilize muscles and joints without restricting the body's range of motion.
